Welcome! You will be given a series of 96 tasks (part 1), another series of 96 tasks (part 2), and then a quick survey (part 3). The whole process should take only about 30 minutes. Note that you should not refresh the page or press back, or else all progress will be lost.

Instructions for part 1

For each task, you will first see a black figure walking left or right for a few seconds. As quickly as possible, press either the ← or → arrow key to choose which way you think it's going. Do this while the black figure is still on screen. In case you mess up, the last direction you press will be the answer we record. Right after the black figure, you will briefly see a white figure either walking or running. After this, please choose whether you think it was walking or running by pressing the corresponding option. You will do 96 tasks like this.

Lastly, throughout each task, there will be a red dot in the center of the screen. Please keep your eyes focused on that red dot at all times while doing the tasks.

When you've understood the instructions and are ready to begin, please fill in your age and gendre, read and agree to the consent form, and then click the "Ready" button. After 1 example task, you'll go straight to doing the 96 tasks of part 1.
